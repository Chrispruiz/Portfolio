About Me

Chris Ruiz


Full Stack Web Developer (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Express.js, Node, SQL)

By the time I was thirteen years old I had already built my first computer part by part and piece by piece. I'd already disassembled and reassembled dozens of computers to troubleshoot issues my friends and family were having with their hardware and software. I've always been fascinated and curious about how computers work and I love the troubleshooting aspect of technology. There is no greater feeling to me than working hard on a project or problem and seeing it all come together in the end. My goal is to create innovative solutions that will help make people's visions come to life.
