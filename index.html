About Me

Chris Ruiz


Front-End Web Developer (HTML and CSS)

By the time I was thirteen years old I had already built my first computer part-by-part. I'd already disassembled and reassembled dozens of computers to troubleshoot issues my friends and family were having with their hardware and software. I've always been fascinated and curious about how computers work and I love the troubleshooting aspect of technology. There is no greater feeling to me than working on hard something and seeing it all come together at the end. My goal is to help your visions become a reality and I won't stop until I get there because I am always chasing that feeling of satisfaction.
